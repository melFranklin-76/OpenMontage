# Editorial Scope

The show covers:

- gay stories
- lesbian stories
- bisexual stories
- Black trans stories
- legacy/icon stories — major LGBTQ movement elders, Stonewall figures, and historically significant activists (e.g., Marsha P. Johnson, Sylvia Rivera, Victoria Cruz, Miss Major, Bayard Rustin, Barbara Gittings, Frank Kameny)

The show avoids:

- generic LGBTQ+ explainer content
- pronoun-debate content
- cis/non-cis discourse
- stories that only mention LGBTQ+ in passing
- general trans stories (unless Black trans lane or legacy/icon lane matches)

## Language guidance

Follow GLAAD's guidance on transgender terminology:
- Use "trans woman," "trans man," "trans activist," "trans rights advocate"
- Never use "a transgender," "transwoman," or "transman"
